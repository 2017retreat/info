Archery

  • - back to top

Want to practice your bulleyes skill with your friends? Register our archery. Do the archery for $5 each person/hour in the afternoon on Saturday! Instructors will be provided by Camp Qwanoes.

Kayaking

  • - back to top

If you'd like to have fun on water with your family and friends during retreat, kayaking is your best option. This year we provide kayaking for $5 each person/hour. Once you register, please don't forget to bring extra cloth to change. Lifeguard will be provided by Camp Qwanoes.

Other Activities

  • - back to top

Besides archery and kayaking, we also have lots of other FREE outdoor activities such pingpong, basketball, volleyball, hiking, etc. Of course, if you just want spend some quality time with God, you can always find a quiet spot during activities time.
