What Must Know

General Dressing Code

No slippers are allowed in any meeting (Please wear socks and shoes)

No mini shorts or skirts are allowed in the camp (Please wear skirt or shorts in acceptable length)

Church Retreat Rules

1. All delegates must register upon arrival.

2. Delegates must be punctual for all events at the Church Retreat.

3. All delegates must return to the dormitory by 11 p.m. and remain quiet while in the dormitory.

4. All delegates are to refrain from entering the dormitory resided by the opposite gender.

5. Please keep the conference center clean and tidy, and remain quiet during meeting time. For any property damage, please notify conference staff immediately.

6. Delegates are not allowed to switch assigned dorm rooms without the consent of the conference staff.

7. Smoking, taking drugs, gambling and consuming alcohol are strictly prohibited in the conference.

8. Delegates are not allowed to bring radio, iPod or MP3 to the conference center.

9. Delegates must put on their name tags at all time.

10. The Church Retreat staff is not responsible for any delegate’s property loss.

11. Any Delegate who violates any of the above rules may have his/her retreat right taken away by the conference staff.

12. The Church Retreat Planning Committee is not responsible for any activity fees during free time.

Other Remarks

1. No water activities allowed except official arrangement

2. No pets allowed
